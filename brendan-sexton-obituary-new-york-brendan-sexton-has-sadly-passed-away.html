Brendan Sexton Obituary, Death – In the heart of New York City, the bustling Sullivan Street mourns the loss of a titan this week. Brendan John Sexton, a stalwart figure of 78 years, bid farewell on November 14, 2023, succumbing to a prolonged illness. Born on June 29, 1945, in Detroit, Michigan, Brendan’s legacy transcends his birthplace and resonates deeply within the fabric of the city he called home.

Brendan’s imprint on New York City is indelible, most prominently as the former NYC Sanitation Commissioner. His groundbreaking initiatives transformed the cityscape, introducing curbside recycling and breaking gender barriers by hiring the first uniformed women sanitation workers in the metropolis. His dedication to public service and community action was not just a professional role but a lifelong commitment.
