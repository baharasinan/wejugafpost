Kylie Jenner, Kim Kardashian in Corset Over T-Shirt: Photos

Posted by Tandra Barner on Friday, September 6, 2024

She got it from her sister! Kylie Jenner has said time and again that she looks up to her older sister Kim Kardashian, so it’s no surprise that she sometimes cribs her style too.

On Monday, October 17, in L.A., Jenner, 19, stepped out in a long white T-shirt, cinched at her tiny waist with an embellished green corset. The Kylie Cosmetics founder added emerald LeSilla boots and that dazzling diamond promise ring from her beau, Tyga.

Think you’re experiencing deja vu? We don’t blame you! Kardashian, 35, wore a nearly identical look while out and about in L.A. in August. The Kimoji creator, who shares two children with husband Kanye West, laced a beige corset over a white tee, though she finished her ensemble with a denim jacket and her warm-weather favorites, Yeezy lucite sandals.

Of course, while Jenner’s love of Kardashian’s style is certainly at play here, it helps that the sisters share a stylist: Monica Rose. “I love working with all of my clients,” Rose, who counts Khloé Kardashian and Chrissy Teigen as clients, told Complex in 2015. “They inspire me, and I love how they all have different styles and allow me to push fashion boundaries and never play it safe. We take risks together and have fun just like you would with your girlfriends.”

Rose also dished on where she finds inspiration for her famous clients: “I [search] Tumblr, older editorials from the ’90s, and I even like digging through all of my old magazines from 10 years ago. I am constantly surfing the Internet looking for new designers to work with.”
