Conjure some congrats! Supernatural star Mark Sheppard is engaged to his girlfriend, Sarah Louise Fudge! 

She shared the happy news on Thursday, Aug. 20, via Twitter with a sweet photo of the actor, 51, sweetly kissing her cheek as she shows off her engagement ring. “Life is good,” she captioned the announcement.

And on Friday, she shared another selfie on Instagram, featuring her stunning new diamond. “Still getting used to this thing!!” she wrote of her new bling.

Fudge revealed in another post that her love had proposed “a couple of weeks ago,” making her the “#happiestgirlintheworld.

Sheppard, who has played the demon Crowley on The CW’s paranormal drama since Season 5, shared that Instagram snap with his followers and captioned it, “Oh yes.”

The English actor, who is the son of actor William Morgan Sheppard, has not been shy about sharing his affection for his ladylove on social media, often posting romantic shots of the couple spending time together.
