I Found the 8 Cutest Spring Tops on Amazon for Less Than $30

Posted by Tandra Barner on Saturday, August 24, 2024

After months of winter chill, sweater season is coming to an end, and we’ll soon have to trade our chunky knits for lightweight blouses. But if your shirt collection could use a refresh, we have just the solution: Amazon curated a section of trendy spring tops, and I narrowed it down to the eight best finds for less than $30. 

The following list includes casual tees, sophisticated button-downs, and everything in between. Plus, with a Prime membership, these tops will arrive at your door step in a matter of days — and I’m all about instant gratification. Browse all eight of our favorite Amazon spring blouses, below, starting at just $19. 

Shop Trending Spring Tops

Amazon Essentials Classic-Fit Long-Sleeve Button-Down Poplin Shirt

A classic button-down shirt is an essential building block of any wardrobe, and shoppers are especially fans of this Amazon Essentials poplin style. Available in nine colors and patterns, the top is made of breezy cotton with a slim-fit silhouette and single chest pocket. Plus, according to a reviewer, it’s a “perfect closet staple” that’s “better-fitting than some of the more expensive brands.” Wear it unbuttoned over a tee and jeans for a casual look or button it up and tuck it into trousers for a sophisticated, office-appropriate outfit. 

Gap Long-Sleeve V-Neck Shirt

If you’re looking for more of a true blouse, go with this Gap long-sleeve V-neck top that’s on sale for $23. It has button cuffs, billowing sleeves, and stylish pleats on the shoulders for movement. And thanks to its timeless shape and silhouette, the blouse can be styled with virtually anything in your closet; roll up the sleeves and wear it casually with jeans, tuck it into high-waisted trousers for an office day, or pair it with a mini skirt for a night out. 

The Drop Mariko Puff-Sleeve Crewneck Stretch Jersey T-Shirt

On sale for $22, this puff-sleeve shirt from The Drop takes your average tee to the next level. Made of a soft and stretchy cotton and spandex blend, the top has elbow-length statement sleeves with elastic cuffs and a body-skimming fit. One reviewer described it as “not too tight but fitted enough,” adding that it’s “flattering, comfortable, and versatile.” Tuck the shirt into jeans, trousers, or a skirt, throw on a lightweight jacket, and you have the perfect spring ‘fit.
