Trailer Park Boys Net Worth 2024

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, August 30, 2024

Trailer park boys is a mockumentary-style Canadian television series starring Rob Wells, John Paul Tremblay and Mike Smith as its main character and directed by Mike Clattenburg. The show is about the blatant residents of Trailer park Ricky, Julian, and Bubbles, who’ll live in a fictional town of Sunnyvale in Dartmouth, Nova, Scotia, trying to make money through illegal ways to get rich quickly. Their plans and practices are pursued by the town’s alcoholic supervisor Jim Lahey and his sidekick Randy.

The series is an extension of Clattenburg’s directed film of the same name, which was premiered on the channel Showcase in 2001. The show ended in the seventh season in 2007, but later in 2013, Rob, John, and Mike purchased the show’s rights and released the rest of its seasons on Netflix. The show’s last season (twelfth) was premiered on 30th March 2018, and its actors Smith claim that its next season will be released soon.

About its Cast

Portraying the character of Ricky LaFleur in Trailer Park Boys Robb Wells is a Canadian actor and screenwriter. Born in the year 1971 in Moncton, New Brunswick Robb has appeared in the film Virginia’s run along with Gabriel Byrne and Joanne Whalley portraying a similar character of Ricky in Trailer Park Boys. He appeared in the third season of the adult sitcom animated series FX Tv series Archer playing the role of a freedom fighter.

Playing the role of Julian John Paul Tremblay, born in 1968 in Dartmouth suburb of Cole Harbour, is a Canadian actor, screenwriter, and comedian. In the trailer Park Boys, he plays the character of a newly released ex-con from jail and coming back to his trailer park in Nova, Scotia. John used to own a pizza restaurant franchise along with his co-star Robb called J.R Capone. He has appeared in the comedy series The Drunk and On Drugs Happy Fun Time Hour in 2010, along with many co-actors of Trailer Park Boys.

Best known for portraying the character of Bubbles in Trailer Park Boys, Michael Smith is a Canadian actor, screenwriter, comedian, and musician born on 27th August 1972 in New Glasgow, Nova Scotia. He is the guitarist of the Canadian rock band Sandbox. He also appeared with his fellow castmates in the comedy series  The Drunk and On Drugs Happy Fun Time Hour.

Net Worth of the Cast Members in 2024

The combined net worth of Trailer Park Boys i.e., John Paul Tremblay, Mike Smith, and Rob Wells, is $6 million with a net worth of $2 million each as of July 2024. They purchased the copyrights from the producers of Trailer Park Boys and released them on their internet streaming network ‘Swearnet’ before releasing it on Netflix.

John Paul has appeared in a total of 10 films and 14 tv series in his career. Before appearing in Trailer Park Boys, he, along with his childhood friend and co-star Rob Wells, owned a chain of Pizza restaurant named J.R Capone’s. He has appeared in super hit movies like ‘The Cart Boy,’ ‘A Hole in One’, and ‘Swearnet: The Movie.

Rob Wells has appeared in a total of 23 films and five tv series in his career. Mike Smith, the third main character of the cast, has appeared in 15 movies and 12 television series in his career. Apart from an actor, he is very successful in his music career. He is the guitarist of the famous rock band of Canada called Sandbox.
