Earnings of Alex Pereira

  • Alex Pereira has had some incredible earnings in his UFC career so far.

  • Another fight that brought in a substantial amount of earnings for Pereira was his match against Sean Strickland on July 2, 2022, in UFC 276.
  • Pereira knocked out Strickland in the first round, and it was voted as the KO of the Month. For this fight, Pereira earned a whopping $188,000.
  • Pereira’s fight against fellow Brazilian Bruno Silva also resulted in some impressive earnings. He won the fight by unanimous decision and made a total of $77,000, including his base salary of $36,000, a fight to win bonus of $36,000, and a fight week incentive of $5,000.
  • For his first fight against Andreas Michailidis at UFC 268, Pereira also earned an impressive $134,000, which included a Performance of the Night Bonus.
