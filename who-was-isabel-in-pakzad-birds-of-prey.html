Who was Isabel in pakzad birds of prey?

Posted by Kelle Repass on Monday, September 9, 2024

Isabel Pakzad is a social media personality, actor, and producer. She is well-known as the girlfriend of actor James Franco.  She was born on January 2, 1993, and she is 27 years old. She was born in New Jersey. She received her education from Tenafly High School.

Pakzad earned a Master’s degree in Integrated Design, Business, and Technology from the University of Southern California.

Isabel is known for Birds of Prey: And the Fantabulous Emancipation of One Harley Quinn (2020), The Deuce (2017), This is 25 and Don’t Worry, It’s Gonna Be OK.

Since November 2017, Isabela has been in a relationship with actor James Franco. According to InStyle, the two began dating in November 2017, but didn’t make their red carpet debut until July 2018 for the premiere of ‘Straight White Men.’

Despite the charges of sexual assault, Pakzad stuck by Franco. Isabel and James have been together for nearly four years, despite keeping their relationship out of the spotlight.
