At What Age Did Shahzada Dawood Die?

Dawood, the son of Hussain Dawood, was born on February 12, 1975, in Rawalpindi, Pakistan and passed away on 18 June 2023 at the age of 48. Azmeh Dawood and her humanitarian sister Sabrina Dawood were his sisters. Abdul Samad Dawood was his brother.

Shahzada Dawood Was the Director of Which Company?

Dawood served as a director of the Dawood Hercules Corporation in addition to serving as vice-chairman of the Engro Corporation. Since 2003, Dawood has served on the board of the Engro Corporation.

In October 2021, he was named vice-chairman of Engro. He had served as the vice-chairman of the Dawood Hercules Corporation prior to this. He has made investments in public-listed companies to identify development prospects through mergers and acquisitions in the textile, food, fertilizer and energy sectors.

Dawood received the World Economic Forum’s Young Global Leaders designation in 2012. In 2020, Dawood gave a speech on the International Day of Women and Girls in Science at the United Nations.

Dawood served as a trustee of the education-focused family foundation The Dawood Foundation (TDF) from 1996 to 2023. The Hussain Dawood Pledge, a private donation to combat COVID-19 in Pakistan, was organized by TDF.

He had concentrated his efforts on helping COVID-19 victims in Pakistan who needed mental health care. In addition, Dawood served as a board member of the SETI Institute, a member of the Global Advisory Board for Prince’s Trust International, a charity established by Charles III and a trustee of the Engro Foundation.
