Jill Marie Jones is an American actress, professional dancer, and former cheerleader who came into prominence after portraying the role of the hilariously self-absorbed Antoinette “Toni” Childs-Garrett on the UPN sitcom series Girlfriends (2000-2006), her performance in it fetching her a nomination for the NAACP Image Award for “Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series” and many other such accolades. She gained further popularity by appearing in numerous other TV shows such as the Starz horror-comedy Ash vs Evil Dead (2015-2016) where she essayed the character of Amanda Fisher. She has been featured in several films as well including Boo! (2018). Jones has amassed more than 400k followers on Instagram.
