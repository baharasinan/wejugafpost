Breakthrough Role: The Bionic Woman

Wagner’s big break came when she was cast as Jaime Sommers in The Bionic Woman, a role that skyrocketed her to fame.

This role significantly boosted her career and solidified her status as a pop culture icon of the 1970s.

FAQs about Lindsay Wagner

Who is Lindsay Wagner?

Lindsay Wagner is an American actress best known for her role as Jaime Sommers in the 1970s TV series The Bionic Woman. She won an Emmy Award for her performance in this role and has had a long and varied career in both television and film.

When is her birthday?

What are some of Lindsay Wagner’s notable achievements?

Besides winning an Emmy for The Bionic Woman, she has been honored with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame and has received the People’s Choice Award for Favorite Female Performer in a New Television Program.
