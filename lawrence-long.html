Lawrence Long or Skip Long is a new contestant who entered the show Jeopardy in season 38 episode 106. Lawrence described himself as a nursing student. Lawrence is yet to discover his long journey in the show which was started in the episode along with Krystle Valerio, and Andrés Quijano. He is on a two-game win streak and it would be interesting to see whether he retain this title on tonight’s episode or not.

Lawrence Long Early Life & Career

Lawrence Long was born in Winston Salem and now residing in East Bend, North Carolina. He studied BA in English from Clemson and currently studying as a medical student at Forsyth Tech Community College. He moved to Chicago after finishing graduate school and started working in a restaurant in Evanston as a wine waiter in 2006 and also studied improv. Lawrence spent a year in New Mexico in 2009-10 working as a sommelier however he moved back to his home when his niece was born in 2011. He retired from restaurants in 2018 and also got certified as an EMT in February 2020. Currently, he is serving as an active member of the Bunker Dogs improv comedy group
