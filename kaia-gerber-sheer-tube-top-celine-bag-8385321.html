Kaia Gerber Made a Case for Carrying the Tube Top Into Fall

Posted by Patria Henriques on Thursday, August 15, 2024

November may be right around the corner, but that is not stopping Kaia Gerber from wearing her summer threads well into fall (this is what happens when you reside in Los Angeles). On Tuesday, the model gave us a break from Halloween content with a selfie of her wearing a sheer gray tube top and white pants, making us nostalgic for warmer temps.

In the image shared to her Instagram, Gerber ran both hands through her hair showing off her sleek Celine by Hedi Slimane Cuir Triomphe Heloise bag, which she paired with a matching cropped tube top. On the bottom, she wore stark white low-rise slacks, breaking that one silly fashion rule that no one follows anyway. Her shoulder-length lob was gentle tousled and parted down the middle, and her no-makeup glam look included dewy skin that glistened in the beaming sunlight.

Over the weekend, Gerber and her boyfriend Austin Butler attended her father Rande Gerber's famous annual Casamigos Halloween party dressed as Andy Warhol and '60s model and actress (and Warhol's muse) Edie Sedgwick. Butler wore a wig replica of Warhol's signature shaggy blonde hairdo while Kaia looked the part in a layered pixie wig, complete with some graphic liner, naturally.

According to People, these specific looks appeared to recreate the professional duo's outfits at the 1965 New York Film Festival. Of course, Gerber's parents, Rande Gerber and Cindy Crawford, were in attendance dressed as Danny Zuko and Sandy Olsson in matching all-black looks. Other A-listers to make appearances at the event included Paris Hilton, Justin Bieber, Megan Fox, Machine Gun Kelly, and many more.
