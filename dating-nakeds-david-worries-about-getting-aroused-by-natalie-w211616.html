Dating Naked's David Worries About Getting Aroused by Natalie

Posted by Patria Henriques on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Eyes up here! Things quickly get awkward on Dating Naked when the two daters strip shortly after introducing themselves in the Wednesday, June 29, season 3 premiere, as seen in a preview clip.

The sexy VH1 dating series’ sneak peek shows Natalie meeting David on a pier in Bora Bora. She teases him about how heavily he is sweating just before they both have to disrobe at the start of the season’s 12-week run, and David is quick to admit that he’s feeling a bit uncomfortable about their atypical arrangement.

“The idea of being naked in front of a complete stranger is something that — hell, I hope she doesn’t laugh!” David tells the camera bluntly. “I don’t want to be laughed at in that situation. I’m stuck, the date’s over!”

Adding to his nerves is the fact that David likes what he sees.

“Looking at Natalie, I’m like, ‘Are you kidding me?'” a clearly impressed David continues. He later calls her “beautiful” and jokes about not wanting to get aroused while talking to his dating partner.

“You got to keep your mind going, you know — six times eight, baseball, all these different things to make sure that, uh, your head is in the right place,” he quips.

For her part, Natalie has no complaints about David’s physique, either: “David’s really hot. He has a really nice jawline, which I’m attracted to, and he has a really nice body. Full package!”

Dating Naked airs on VH1 Wednesdays at 9 p.m. ET.
