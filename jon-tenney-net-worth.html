What is Jon Tenney's Net Worth?

Jon Tenney is an American actor who has a net worth of $6 million. Jon Tenney was born in Princeton, New Jersey in December 1961. In 1988 he starred as Feke on the television series The Dirty Dozen. Tenney starred as Peter Bauer on the TV series Equal Justice from 1990 to 1991. From 1997 to 1998 he starred as Patrol Sgt. Francis X. Donovan on the series Brooklyn South. Jon Tenney starred as Tommy Ballantine on the television series Kristin in 2001.

From 2005 to 2012 he starred as Fritz Howard on the TV series The Closer. Tenney starred as Sean King on the series King & Maxwell in 2013 and as Andrew Nichols on the series Scandal from 2014 to 2016. From 2015 to 2017 he starred as Nick Tramble on the television series Hand of God and from 2012 to 2018 he starred as Fritz Howard on the TV series major Crimes. Tenney starred as Alan Jones on the series True Detective in 2019. He has starred in several films including Tombstone, Beverly Hills Cop III, Free Willy 2: The Adventure Home, Fools Rush In, Green Lantern, I See You, and more. He has been nominated for five Screen Actors Guild Awards for The Closer. Jon was married to Teri Hatcher from 1994 to 2003.
