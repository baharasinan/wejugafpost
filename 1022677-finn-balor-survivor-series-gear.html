Finn Blor Comments On Survivor Series Gear: I Did NOT Enjoy Wearing A T-Shirt

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Many viewers of WWE Survivor Series commented on the fact that RAW and SmackDown team members (aside from a select few) all had to wear matching outfits during their matches. One person that was not a fan of the gear was RAW Survivor’s Series team member Finn Bálor. However, it’s not for the reasons you may have thought.

Bálor’s criticism is two-fold. One, his red shoes were too slippery, and led to a pretty funny moment where he fell on top of the Miz. Secondly, Finn wasn’t happy with wearing a shirt. After all, he clearly works hard to have the abs of a God, and he should get to show them off whenever he wants!
