Julianne Moore, Husband Bart Freundlich, Lookalike Kids in Mexico: Pic

Posted by Tandra Barner on Wednesday, September 4, 2024

Moore family fun in Mexico! Four-time Oscar-nominated actress Julianne Moore enjoyed a Mexican beach getaway with her hunky husband Bart Freundlich and their lookalike kids last weekend!

The 53-year-old actress, who wore black shorts, a gray sweater and scarf wrapped around her neck, covered her flaming red hair with a white cap as she walked along the shore with her adorable family. Freundlich strolled beside Moore with his arm draped across his wife's shoulder, while son Caleb, 16, and daughter Liv, 11, splashed and ran ahead of their parents.

 

During the fun excursion, Moore stripped down to her bikini bottoms as her shirtless hubby guided her into the wave-filled waters of the beach. The fit mother of two kept her long locks up in a loose bun as she ventured into the rocky waters breaking along the coast — and covered her lean figure up top in a long-sleeved wetsuit shirt.
