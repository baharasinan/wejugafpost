What Is Kyle Richards' Net Worth

Posted by Tandra Barner on Friday, September 6, 2024

The Real Housewives franchise is built on the backbone of the luxury and wealth the ladies in the shows have to offer. Kyle Richards has been the wealthiest cast member on the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills and the entire franchise for a long time now.

However, her sister Kathy Hilton’s addition to the cast albeit temporarily throws Richards off her throne. But just how much is Richards’s worth?

Richards held the throne for the wealthiest cast member until her sister’s arrival

Aside from acting, Richards is also an influential businesswoman with several clothing lines, such as NYFW. Ever since she joined RHOBH, Richards’s net worth has increased significantly. Her business ventures and acting career have earned her an impressive net worth. Although Richards’ net worth is in the millions, many publications usually combine her wealth alongside her husband’s.

Richards’ husband Mauricio Umansky is a celebrated property agent. He has been involved with various high-selling properties such as the Playboy Mansion and Walt Disney Estate through his real estate firm, The Agency. He has also sold properties to multiple celebrities such as Michael Jackson and Prince. With a combined wealth estimation, Richards is worth a whopping $100 million.

However, Celebrity Net Worth reports that Richard’s sister is worth a lot more with a net worth of $350 million which is primarily thanks to her tycoon husband, Ricky Hilton of the Hilton hotels. Hilton owns a real estate agency, Hilton & Hyland, with an impressive $85 million sale of the largest residential sale in Los Angeles, the Candy Spelling estate.

How the other Housewives rank

When put in comparison with other cast members, Richards’s net worth seems monstrous. However, other cast members are also equally wealthy, although not nearly as rich as Hilton or Richards. Dorit Kemsley has a combined net worth of $50 million with her husband P.K.

Lisa Rinna ties in with Garcelle Beauvais with an estimated net worth of about $8 million, while Erika Jayne is reportedly worth $15 million. Erika has recently found herself in legal entanglements and a pending divorce which might foreseeably alter her financial status. Crystal Minkoff and her director husband have an estimated net worth of $16 million.

Sutton Stracke probably has the least wealth of all the women, with sources reporting that she sits at around $2 million. Although when compared to other people, it may seem a lot, Stracke’s net worth is far less than her cast members and a far cry from Rinna’s statement about Stracke’s finances.

Richards started as a child actor

Richards was born in Los Angeles in January 1969 to Kenneth Edwin and Kathleen Mary Richards, who named her after Kyle Rote of the New York Giants. Richards’ parents separated in 1972, and her mother remarried twice more. Richards has five half-siblings, two of whom are well known to the public, Kathy and Kim.

The reality star began acting in 1974 with Little House On The Prairie. Kim and Richards appeared on screen together in the 1977 film The Car. Throughout the ’70s Richards enjoyed success with Flying High, Fantasy Island, and Carter Country.

Since then, Richards has appeared in various productions like The Watcher In The Woods, ER as Nurse Dori Kerns, Deadly Sibling Rivalry, The Hungover Games, Love Boat, CSI, City Guys, Seventh Heaven and Beverly Hills,90210. Richards joined the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills in 2010 and has stayed with the show since then.

Additionally, the actor executive produced American Woman, a Paramount Network comedy series based loosely on her childhood. As of 2021, Richards is slated to appear in Halloween Kills, reprising her role as Lindsey Wallace. She will also appear in the Real Housewives All-Stars spinoff.
