Claudia Jordan Won't Return for Real Housewives of Atlanta Season 8

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Kenya Moore's friend is out! Claudia Jordan is just the latest star to say goodbye to Bravo. The TV/radio host won't be returning to the Real Housewives of Atlanta for Season 8, a source confirms to Us Weekly.

Jordan, 42, joined the franchise just last season. But as viewers very well know (cat fight alert!) she didn't get along with vet NeNe Leakes. She often stood up to the Dancing With the Stars alum, and didn't sit back when Leakes at one point called her a half-breed.

"When you were my age, you had edges!" Jordan yelled during one memorable argument. Leakes snapped back: "You've been f–d by everybody in Hollywood. They done wore that out. What you got, this half-breed s–t? That's not in Atlanta. They ain't looking for you. You're a whore, you done slept with everybody.
