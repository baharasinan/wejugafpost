Phil Wright Choreography

Phil Wright was born November 12th, 1986 and grew up in Miami, Florida. At age 9, he started dancing under the direction of Steve Cruz at the African Heritage Cultural Art Center.

Phil Wright Choreographer

He now is currently active as a professional dancer and choreographer. Phil has danced for artists including Will Smith, Chayanne, Nicki Minaj, and Pitbull. He also is a teacher for Millennium Dance Complex, Debbie Reynolds Dance Studio, IDA Dance Academy, and Abby Lee Dance Studio.

Phil Wright Age

Phil is aged 33 years old as of 2019.

Phil Wright Wife

Wright proposed to his longtime girlfriend of 8 years, fellow dancer Ashley Liai while they were performing a set to John Legend’s “You & I Nobody In The World.” At the end f the performance, Wright got down on one knee and asked Ashley to be his wife.

Phil Wright Dance Classes

Wright would dance at school talent shows and be introduced to street hip hop when he joined the dance group the Skool Boys. Wright would dip into choreography and would choreograph the high school dance team and later became a Hip hop dance teacher at a small studio in Homestead, FL, the Paramount Dance Studios.

He travels as much as he can teaching new students in new areas spreading his knowledge and love of dance promo video.

Question: What inspired your passion for dance?

 Wright: My passion for dance was inspired by one of the top hip-hop performers in the ’80s and early ’90s; MC Hammer!

Question: Can you share your earliest dance memory, with us?

 Wright: One of my first dance experiences was when I was randomly dancing at my sister’s reception after her wedding, I was 9 years old.

Question: How have you enjoyed your time in Australia?

Wright: Australia has been such an exciting environment with so much to explore. It has definitely exceeded all of my expectations, such as the talent in dancers, nature and the gratitude from all the students here.

Question: Can you tell us about the Australian dance classes, you ran?

 Wright: All the Australian students expected life-changing lessons that they will never forget. I’m here to change lives.

Question: How did the idea for the proposal video, come about?

Phil Wright: I thought about proposing to my fiancé about two weeks prior to the big day. It all made sense because the dance floor is where we met.

Question: Can you share any wedding details, with us?

Phil Wright: As of now, the wedding is set for January 12th, 2019 to be held in Miami, Florida.

Question: Who are you currently listening and dancing to?

Phil Wright: Currently the genre of music that I listen to is endless, I dance to any and everything that moves my heart.

Question: What’s a typical day like, for you?

Phil Wright: My days actually run in together, as of late, and I absolutely love it. Waking up at the crack of dawn and not going to sleep until  2 am/3 am is everything that I wished for. Busy is better than bored!
