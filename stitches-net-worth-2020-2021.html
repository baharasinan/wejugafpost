Early Life

Born in the picturesque city of Miami, Florida, Stitches was named Phillip Nickolas Katsabanis at birth. Esther and Alexander Katsabanis already had two sons named Dimitri and Alexander when their third child, Stitches, was born. Shortly afterward, the couple split up, and the children were brought up by their mother, who supported the family working as a health insurance broker.

Growing up, Stitches was very inclined towards singing and dancing and often was at the center of his family gatherings. At about 14 years of age, he was dismissed from the G. A self-made man, Stitches now solely concentrates on his musical career although, he does rap a lot about his life experiences as a drug dealer. On the personal front, he settled into married life in 2012 and now is the proud father of 3 kids.
