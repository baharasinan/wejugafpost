Westside Gunn Shows Off Newborn Baby Daughter In Red Maybach Truck: God Is The Greatest

Posted by Patria Henriques on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Westside Gunn welcomed a newborn baby girl into the world recently. Gunn showed off his daughter in an Instagram post on Thursday (February 25) with A.J. riding in style in the backseat of his luxurious red Maybach truck.

“GOD IS THE GREATEST,” he captioned his post. “Even tho she’s a girl she’s my junior Welcome to this krazy world “AJ” aka “WESTSIDE JR” #BLESSINGS.”

The A in AJ may have something to do with Westside Gunn’s real first name being Alvin. In the clip, the Griselda rapper records the red Maybach truck, which he claims to be one of the only ones existing in the entire world.

“Yeah, it’s the only red Maybach truck, but more important than that, we got a baby,” Gunn says while showing off his daughter in a higher-pitched voice.

For those familiar with Westside Gunn’s discography, they’ve probably heard his other daughter — who goes by Westside Pootie — lay vocals on other tracks.
