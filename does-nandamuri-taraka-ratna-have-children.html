Death

He took part in a political road show event in Kuppam, Andhra Pradesh, on January 27, 2023, alongside his cousin Nara Lokesh. Ratna went into a severe heart attack and lost consciousness during the incident. He received first assistance and cardio pulmonary resuscitation (CPR).

He was treated in a neighbourhood hospital in Kuppam before being sent the next day to a Bangalore hospital with a focus on cardiac care. Two days prior to his passing, he received treatment from heart doctors from the USA, including the operations of balloon angioplasty, intra aortic balloon pump, vasoactive support, and other cutting-edge cardiac services. Ratna passed away on February 18, 2023, in Bangalore while receiving medical care at the age of 39.
