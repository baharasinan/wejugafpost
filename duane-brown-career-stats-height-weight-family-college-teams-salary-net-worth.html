Brown’s professional career boasts an impressive 16 seasons, highlighting his unwavering commitment and longevity in the NFL. He has consistently demonstrated his dominance as an offensive tackle, amassing impressive statistics:

College, Draft, Teams Played:

Brown’s talent was evident early on, leading him to excel on the football field at Virginia Tech University. He further honed his skills and established himself as a dominant offensive lineman, paving the way for an illustrious professional career.

Houston Texans (2008-2017)

Seattle Seahawks (2017-present)
